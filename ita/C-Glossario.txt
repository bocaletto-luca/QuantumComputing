## Appendice C: Bibliografia e risorse online

### C.1 Testi fondamentali  
- Nielsen M. A., Chuang I. L., *Quantum Computation and Quantum Information*, Cambridge University Press, 2000  
- Preskill J., *Lecture Notes for Physics 229: Quantum Information and Computation*, Caltech, 1998–2000  
- Rivas Á., Huelga S. F., *Open Quantum Systems: An Introduction*, Springer, 2012  
- Shor P. W., “Polynomial-Time Algorithms for Prime Factorization and Discrete Logarithms”, *SIAM Journal on Computing*, 1997  

### C.2 Articoli di riferimento  
- Farhi E., Goldstone J., Gutmann S., *A Quantum Approximate Optimization Algorithm*, arXiv:1411.4028, 2014  
- Harrow A. W., Hassidim A., Lloyd S., “Quantum Algorithm for Linear Systems of Equations”, *Physical Review Letters*, 2009  
- Peruzzo A. et al., “A Variational Eigenvalue Solver on a Photonic Quantum Processor”, *Nature Communications*, 2014  
- Kjaergaard M. et al., “Superconducting Qubits: Current State of Play”, arXiv:2005.12667, 2020
